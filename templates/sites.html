On training days we meet at Abingdon Services on the M74 at 10:30am for a quick coffee and to look at the paperwork required for the days activities.

We will discuss the weather briefly and how we see it progressing throughout the day, as it is a vital part of the overall consideration to fly. From the service station, we then drive to the designated flight area.
